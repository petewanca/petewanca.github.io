ABOUT

Hey there. I'm Pete and I'm a mixing and mastering engineer located in Nashville, Tennessee, USA. I've spent my whole life in and around music and my experience enables me to have a unique perspective on the creative process.

I specialize in working with artists to understand their vision and create an end product they can be proud of -- great sounding music to share with the world.

If you want to work with an engineer who will not only listen to you but also push you to create the best possible result, then let's chat and see if we're a good fit for each other!
