About

I've spent my whole life in and around music. I've worked as a player, tech, recording engineer, and mixing engineer. My focus and service is mix engineering.

I only offer mix engineering services because (1) its my favorite way to work with musicians and (2) narrowing my focus enables me to offer a better end product to my clients.

I will work with you to understand the vision for your art help create something you can be proud of.
